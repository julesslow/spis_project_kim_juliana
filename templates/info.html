About NekoNote!

NekoNote is a web-based rhythm game where you can upload your own music and create custom note patterns. Enjoy playing to your favorite tunes!

How to Play

  • Upload a music file (MP3 or WAV).
  • Wait for the AI model to generate beatmaps based on your file.
  • Play the game by hitting notes when they pop up.

Controls

  • Use Q and P to hit notes.
